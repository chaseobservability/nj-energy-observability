## A. Executive Snapshot (<= 6 bullets)

Purpose: orient leadership in under 2 minutes.
- System posture (contextual, non-forecast)
- Peak pressure vs recent baseline (direction only)
- Capacity exposure (descriptive band)
- Emissions in risk hours (if available)
- EO1 milestone movement (if any)
- Data caveats (only if necessary)

Rules:
- No numbers without context
- No causality claims
- No "why" -- only "what changed"

## B. EO1 Implementation Status (Public Artifacts Only)

Purpose: make EO1 observable, not debatable.
- Table of EO1 milestones
- Status based solely on published documents
- Links only; no interpretation

## C. Scorecard Highlights (Zone-First)

Purpose: surface where attention might be warranted.

Sections:
- Load & Peak Behavior
- Emissions in Risk Hours (if available)
- Affordability (Capacity-related exposure only)

Rules:
- Zones first, statewide second
- "Outlier vs baseline" language only
- No rankings, no winners/losers

## D. Signals -> Questions (Core Value)

Purpose: turn data into better inquiry, not conclusions.

For each signal:
- Signal (Outcome/Program): factual observation
- Question: clarifying, non-leading, answerable via public info

Rules:
- Questions only
- No verbs like "should," "expect," "likely"
